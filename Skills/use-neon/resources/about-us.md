Our mission: deliver Postgres as a cloud service designed to help teams build scalable, dependable applications faster than ever.

Neon is built on a distributed architecture that separates storage and compute, unlocking the level of performance, reliability, and scale to make Postgres a foundational building block as universal as S3.

## Where we're headed

[Neon is a Databricks company.](https://www.databricks.com/company/newsroom/press-releases/databricks-agrees-acquire-neon-help-developers-deliver-ai-systems) In May 2025, Neon joined Databricks to shape the future of Postgres and AI-native development. The mission stays the same: deliver Postgres for developers and AI agents — now backed by the scale and expertise of Databricks.

[The same technology behind Neon powers Lakebase:](https://www.databricks.com/blog/announcing-lakebase-public-preview) The first serverless Postgres database integrated with the lakehouse, built for the AI era.
